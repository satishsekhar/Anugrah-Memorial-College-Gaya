THE COLLEGE :

Anugrah Memorial College, Gaya is a uniqe institution of higher education in Bihar. The College was established in 1964 and converted into a constituent unit of Magadh University, Bodh-Gaya in 1980. The College is situated in the heart of Gaya town in an area over 23 acres of land. It has a number of double-storeyed buildings accommodating the faculties of Management, Science, Social Sciences, Humanities and Law. The College is managed and maintained as per law under the authority of the Goverment of Bihar and the Cjancellor of the Universities of Bihar, Its all department inculiding the Department of Business Administration are directly governed by the Magadh University, Bodh-Gaya through its authoritative statutory bodies.
